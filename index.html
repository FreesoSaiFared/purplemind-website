Welcome to PurpleMind

We are currently building a new forum dedicated to supporting individuals who have experienced long-term effects after ketamine use.

At PurpleMind, we aim to create a safe and inclusive space for individuals to connect, share experiences, and access resources. Our platform will feature:

  • An open forum for general discussions
  • A private forum for more sensitive conversations
  • AI moderation tools to ensure a safe and respectful community

PurpleMind is designed to support those dealing with psychological or physical challenges, including:

  • Depression
  • Pain
  • Persistent mental or emotional effects

We see ourselves as veterans who are paving the way for safer practices and better understanding of psychedelic realities. Together, we can turn challenges into opportunities for growth.
