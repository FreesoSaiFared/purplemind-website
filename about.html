Our Mission

PurpleMind.info exists to provide accurate, unbiased information about ketamine while respecting individual autonomy and personal choices. We are not a rehabilitation center or recovery organization - we are an information platform dedicated to harm reduction and understanding.

What We Are

  • An information resource for individuals seeking to understand ketamine's effects
  • A platform for sharing both scientific research and personal experiences
  • Advocates for harm reduction and informed decision-making
  • A judgment-free space for open dialogue

What We Are Not

  • A rehabilitation or recovery center
  • Medical professionals offering treatment
  • Advocates for or against ketamine use
  • A substitute for professional medical advice

Our Approach

We believe in:

  • Respecting individual autonomy and the right to make informed choices about one's own body and consciousness
  • Providing accurate information without judgment or bias
  • Acknowledging both mainstream medical perspectives and alternative viewpoints
  • Supporting harm reduction through education and awareness
  • Creating a safe space for sharing experiences and seeking information

Our Commitment

We are committed to:

  • Maintaining up-to-date, accurate information
  • Respecting privacy and personal choices
  • Providing resources for emergency situations
  • Bridging the gap between medical knowledge and user experiences
  • Supporting the free-choosing individual's right to information
